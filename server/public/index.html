Hello there!

For my project, I made a Google Chrome extention for cross referencing scriptures in the standard works.

How To Use

  1. Download the extension.
  2. Pin the extension by right clicking the "puzzle piece" in the top right corner of your browser and selecting the "pin" icon next to the "Cross Reference Assist" Extension.
  3. Navigate to churchofJesusChrist.org and find your favorite scripture.
  4. Highlight a portion you would like to cross reference.
  5. Click the "Cross Reference Assist" Extension icon that now appears next to the "puzzle piece" icon in the top right corner of your browser.
  6. Wait 30 seconds to a minute. Your results will appear as an "alert" pop-up.

Sometimes chat will provide more information than can fit in this alert box. I'm sorry if it gets cut off. If you really want, come find me and I can get the rest of your response from the server logs.
